Lo Mein Noodles Recipes

Description

The following recipe is for making the Lo Mein Noodles.

These lo mein noodles were created from a blend of multiple recipes. Add your favorite meat for a main dish or serve as a side. If serving with meat, cook the meat separately.

Ingredients

You will need the following items in order to make the dish:

  • 1 pack of Spaghetti
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons teriyaki sauce
  • 2 table spoon honey
  • 1/4 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 stalks celery; sliced
  • 2 large carrots, cut into large matchsticks
  • 1/2 sweet onion; thinly sliced
  • 2 green onions; sliced

Steps

Please follow the steps in order given below:

  1. Gather all Ingredients
  2. Boiling the Spaghetti
    1. Take a large pot of slightly salted water to a boil.
    2. Cook the spaghetti in boiling water until it becomes tender yet firm.
    3. After around 12 minutes drain and rinse with cold water to cool.
  3. Whisk together soy sauce, teriyaki sauce, honey, and ginger in a small bowl.
  4. Frying the spaghetti
    1. Heat oil in a larger skillet or wok over high heat
    2. Firstly cook and stir celery, carrots, onion and green onions in hot oil.
    3. after around 5 to 7 minutes andd the spaghetti and soy sauce mixture.
  5. Cook stirring frequently, until heated through, about 5 minutes.

Enjoy!!
